# Architecture Decision Records

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture important architectural decisions made during the development of PostgreSQL Partition Manager, along with their context and consequences.

## What is an ADR?

An ADR documents a significant decision that affects the structure, behavior, or qualities of the system. Each record describes the context, decision, and consequences so that future contributors understand why the project is designed the way it is.
